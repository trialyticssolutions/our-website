Round 2 — three fresh grounds.

Round 1 leaned on the standard studio-site hues (cool near-black, petrol, off-white + cobalt). These three break from that: a deep evergreen with brass, a flat electric ultramarine used as color-block, and a warm tungsten dark. None of these hue families appear in AI-template output, and none are common among data consultancies. Hover buttons and rows — motion character is live.

Motion personality — "Composed"

Unhurried 500–700ms easings, generous stagger. Sections settle into place; brass hairlines draw in as dividers. Hovers are slow background sweeps — the site never rushes you, the way expensive things don't.

Scroll & 3D

A data topography: contour lines in brass wire forming a rising landscape — raw data as terrain being surveyed. It elevates and rotates gently with scroll, each service section illuminating a different ridge.

Signature element

Brass contours — the topographic line motif recurs as section dividers and behind metrics, giving the whole site the character of a beautifully drawn survey map.

The inversion — signature moment

Ready to transform your data into your competitive advantage?

Mid-scroll, the page inverts: chalk becomes ultramarine edge-to-edge, type flips to chalk, and the CTA lands inside the boldest moment on the site. This band is a live sample of that treatment.

Motion personality — "Kinetic"

Confident and architectural: 300–450ms with sharp ease-out. Headlines slide in as whole blocks, color-block sections wipe over each other on scroll. Hovers are ink sweeps and hard color flips — graphic, not glossy.

Scroll & 3D

A white ceramic-and-glass abstract form floating on the ultramarine hero block — matte, art-directed, single key light. It fractures into an ordered grid of cubes as you scroll into services (raw → structured), each service snapping a layer into alignment.

Signature element

The inversion — the full-bleed flip from chalk to ultramarine. Visitors remember the site as "the blue one," which is exactly the kind of recall a brand accent is for.

Motion personality — "Workshop"

Precise 300–420ms movements with a slight mechanical settle at the end, like a tool locking into its fixture. Amber indicator dots blink on as sections become active. Hovers are lifts with an amber edge-light catching the element.

Scroll & 3D

A machined metal assembly — an abstract precision part in dark steel with warm amber key lighting — that rotates and disassembles into its components as you scroll, each service section holding one component in focus, then reassembling at the CTA.

Signature element

The filament line — a thin amber thread that glows along section edges and under active nav items, like a heated wire. Warm light on dark metal becomes the brand's texture.
